About RT Hotel

"Where every guest is treated like royalty."

RT Hotel is a premier hospitality destination located in the heart of Mbarara town, Western Uganda. Since opening our doors, we have been dedicated to offering an experience that combines genuine Ugandan warmth with modern comfort and convenience.

Whether you are a business traveler seeking a quiet and productive space, a couple on a romantic getaway, or a family looking for a comfortable base to explore western Uganda — RT Hotel has something special waiting for you.

Our on-site restaurant serves freshly prepared meals using locally sourced ingredients, and our bar offers a relaxing space to unwind after a long day. From our well-equipped gym to our peaceful gardens and spa, we ensure your stay goes beyond just a room.
